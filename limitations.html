Limitations

1. If something goes wrong, refresh the page by clicking on F5 on your keyboard
2. We're currently showing duplicate entried in Words Found when ticking the "Find whole words" checkbox.
2. Duplicates in Words Found in Text: the same word might appear with a capital letter and without, e.g. "video" and "Video".
3. This is an ongoing project, so if something does not work, or does not work as expected, or is not updated, email me: oharel@gmail.com
    I promise to look at it when I have time.
4. If typing words manually, use one per line and lower case only. Clicking on "Look'em up" will duplicate the words and replace the first letter of each word with a capital letter.
5. We're currently finding whole words as part of other words. For instance: 'low' in 'fellow'. This is useful when suffixes
    are missing ('talk' vs 'talks'), but less so in the case of 'low'-'fellow', especially if the list of words contains the word 'fellow'.
999. Use at your own discretion. This means that I'm not responsible bla bla bla...
